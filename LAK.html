A work-in-progress diplomatic edition of Deimel’s Liste der archaischen Keilschriftzeichen von Fara, based on the Oracc-LAK font by Steve Tinney.

Artefact designations are linked to CDLI; locations within the artefact are linked to the appropriate location in an Oracc transliteration. The text can viewed side-by-side with a facsimile for proofreading, or reflowed from its original lines for ease of reading on screens of various sizes.

The Neo-Assyrian font is 𒀭𒀝𒌷𒀏𒄴𒋢𒊻.
